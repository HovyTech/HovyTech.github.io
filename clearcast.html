ClearCast
Description 
A beautiful designed weather app that provides accurate information for your current location. The app is also capable of working offline by remembering your weather conditions for the current, hourly and daily state. 

Control a 3D projection of the Earth and Moon. Observe the Moons surface in high resolutions and see the Earth orientate itself to your position on the Globe. 

An Apple Watch App and Today Widget was put together so that it's quick and easy to see what the current conditions are. 

Detailed five day forecasts provide accurate information. Live icons have been integrated into the app and change their appearance as the hours pass. 

Hourly Graphs have been designed to show a full overview of a 24-hour forecast. It has been structured to portray the information in a clean and easy way to understand. 

ClearCast Includes: 
- Today Widget 
- Apple Watch App 
- Offline Functionality 
- Current Conditions 
- Hourly Forecast 
- 5 Day Forecast 

Weather Information Includes: 
- Current Temp

- Min Temp

- Feels Like 
- Cloud Cover 
- Humidity 
- Precipitation 
- Dew Point 
- Wind Speed 
- Wind Direction 
- Storm Distance 
- Storm Direction 
- Pressure 
- Ozone 
- Sunrise & Sunset 
- Moon Phase 
- Current Location 

Hourly Graphs Include: 
- Temperature 
- Feels Like 
- Precipitation 
- Humidity

- Daily Temps
